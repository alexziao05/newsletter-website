What If Audit Guide

Learn how to explore different majors and minors using SDSU's "What If" audit tool

❓ What is a "What If" Audit?
"What If" audits are degree evaluations run under a different major. This means you can change the major or minor in which your coursework is evaluated and the results will display the remaining requirements you would have if you were to change your major or minor.
⚠️ Does running my "What If Audit" declare or change my major?
NO. This tool is designed to help undeclared students OR students who want to switch majors or declare a minor, to see what course requirements they must complete.
✅ Important: This does not declare your major or minor.
1

Open your degree evaluation in your my.sdsu account

Log in to your my.sdsu account and navigate to the degree evaluation section. You'll find this under the Academic Tools menu.

2

Click on the "What If" Audit button

Look for the "What If" button in your degree evaluation interface. This will open the audit tool where you can explore different academic pathways.

3

Select your desired major or minor

Choose the major or minor you're interested in exploring from the dropdown menu. You can run multiple scenarios to compare different options.

4

Review your results

Analyze the audit results to see which requirements you've already completed and what courses you still need. Use this information to make informed decisions about your academic path.
